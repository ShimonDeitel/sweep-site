Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

The short version: Sweep is built to run entirely on your iPhone. It collects no personal data, uses no third-party trackers or analytics SDKs, and has no server that your photos are ever sent to. Your library never leaves your device.

1. Our approach to privacy

Sweep ("the app", "we", "us") is a photo-cleaning utility for iPhone. We designed it on a simple principle: a tool that touches your entire photo library should never be able to see, copy, or upload it. Everything Sweep does happens locally on your device.

2. Data we collect

None. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Specifically, Sweep does not collect:

  • Your photos, videos, or any content from your library.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • Account information — Sweep has no accounts and no sign-up.
  • Location data, device identifiers for tracking, or advertising IDs.
  • Usage analytics, behavioral data, or crash telemetry sent to us.

3. How Sweep uses photo access

To do its job, Sweep asks for permission to access your Photos through Apple's standard iOS permission prompt. This access is used only on your device and only to:

  • Display your photos and videos so you can swipe through them.
  • Analyze photos locally (for Pro's smart finders such as duplicates, similar shots, blurry photos, and large videos).
  • Move photos you choose to bin into the iOS Recently Deleted album when you tap Clean.

Photos analyzed by Sweep are never copied off your device. You can review or revoke photo access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos on your iPhone.

4. Deleting photos is recoverable

Sweep never permanently deletes anything on its own. When you tap Clean, the photos you binned are moved to your device's standard Recently Deleted album, where iOS keeps them for 30 days before removing them. During that window you can recover any photo directly in the Apple Photos app.

5. No third parties

Sweep includes no third-party advertising networks, no analytics or attribution SDKs, and no social-media trackers. We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

6. Purchases

Sweep Pro is a one-time in-app purchase. Payments are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. We never see or store your payment details, card numbers, or Apple Account information. Apple may process purchase data under Apple's Privacy Policy. Restoring your purchase is handled by Apple and requires no account with us.

7. Network use

Sweep is designed to work fully offline. The app does not send your content over the network. The only network activity is the App Store purchase or restore flow, which is handled by Apple's systems, not ours.

8. Children's privacy

Sweep does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Sweep handles data, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@example.com and we'll be happy to help.
