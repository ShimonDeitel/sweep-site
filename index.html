FAQ

Questions, answered

Is it safe to use?

Yes. Sweep runs entirely on your iPhone and never deletes anything without your tap. Binned photos move to iOS Recently Deleted, where they wait 30 days before being removed — so nothing is lost by accident.

Does it delete photos permanently?

No. When you tap Clean, photos go to your standard iOS Recently Deleted album. You have 30 days to recover anything before iOS removes it permanently — exactly like deleting in the Photos app.

Can you see my photos?

No. Your photos never leave your iPhone — there is no photo upload and no server we could view them from. The only thing stored off-device is your private Sign in with Apple ID and your Pro unlock status, kept in your own iCloud (Apple CloudKit).

Is it really a one-time purchase?

Yes. The core app is free with no caps. Sweep Pro is a single $0.99 in-app purchase through the App Store — no subscriptions, no recurring charges. Restore it for free on your other devices.

Does it need an internet connection?

Photo cleaning works fully offline. The only network activity is the App Store purchase or restore and the CloudKit sync of your Pro status — both handled by Apple, never a server of ours.

Which iPhones does it support?

Sweep is built for modern iPhones running a recent version of iOS. If your phone runs the current or previous major iOS release, you're good to go.
